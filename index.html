Getting Started FAQs Helpline
Welcome to the official website of the Sarkaar Sahyog Portal by the Ministry Of Personnel, Public Grievances & Pensions, Government of India! This portal has been designed to provide comprehensive, reliable, user-friendly and one-stop source of registering complaints, only for the citizens of India. If you are visiting this website for the first time and are intending to register a complaint, you may spend a few minutes in reading this advisory.
  • How much time will my complaint take to be processed?
    • It depends on the complaint type and complexity. Usually, it takes 3-5 working days.
  • What can a citizen do if he is not satisfied with the redressal of his complaint?
    • User can submit feedback on the feedback page.
  • How do I track my complaint status?
    • The user can check the status of their complaint on the Complaint Status page.
